Corpus Analysis Assignment

The screen captures below show my findings between Edgar Allan Poe's works and Bram Stocker's Dracula. The Voyant word clusters when compared to each other have many similar words including "said", "time", and "like". Upon further investigation using Antconc's KWIC, both texts often use the word "like" to compare two things together in the form of a simile. I also found that Edgar Allan Poe uses repetition in his works to add suspense and put an enphasis on the madness that occurs throughout his stories. Bram Stocker however, focuses more on descriptive words to paint a vivid picture in the readers mind for them to expereince stages of grief and fear.
