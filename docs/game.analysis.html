Game Analysis Style Savvy: Trendsetters

Game Overview

Style Savvy: Trendsetters is a fashion simulation game, the main goal is to provide customers with all their clothing needs while becoming a fashion superstar. This game served as a great pastime for me throughout the course of this assignment. Not only is this game very fun to play, but it also allows for the expression of creativity by becoming a "fashion designer". I loved how simple yet complex this game could be with all of its different elements.


The Game World

The features included in the map of the game help create the illusion of exploring a world. The different locations, including My Apartment, My Shop, Buyer’s Center, Downtown, etc., allow the player to experience visiting different areas of the map while providing location-specific features. The game also provides helpful commentary that walks you through these features after you progress throughout the game and unlock new locations. The My Apartment location on the map is, I believe, a really significant part of the game that makes the player feel more immersed in the gameplay. Having a location specifically designed as a home for your character creates a more realistic world, as it really feels like you are going "home" after a long day selling clothes at your shop. In My Apartment, you are able to change your character's clothing and decorate your room with items like furniture and wallpaper. This allows for the player’s creativity to emerge, as they can customize their character to their liking with the immense amount of clothing pieces and room decor. When I get on the game, the first thing I do is visit My Apartment and create a new look for my character during my play session. I enjoy being able to not only style clothing for customers but also for myself.

Characters

Style Savvy: Trendsetters is played from the point of view of your own character, in which you control where she goes and what she does. Your character interacts with other characters in the form of text dialogue. The game follows one storyline, so rather than the player having dialogue of their own, the player sees or listens to the automated dialogue of NPCS. There are a variety of different characters in the game; you get to know them by talking to them, during which they display their unique personalities. Interactions with NPCs are also automated; the completion of certain events will bring up dialogue with certain characters, advancing the storyline. In regards to the main NPC characters like Michaela and Emmylou, the game provides you with dialogue that lets the player get to know their special interests and motives. Although some of the dialogue can get a bit repetitive, it really helps form a better connection between you and the NPCS.

Challenges

In this game, you are challenged as a player by being able to provide the clothing your customers are looking for. If you provide the customers with clothing that fits the specific style they are asking for, they will buy the item. However, if you fail to do this, the customer becomes disappointed and leaves. You need to strategically buy your inventory and maintain a variety of different clothing items and styles in your shop to keep your business successful. You make progress in the game by serving customers and winning contests at the contest hall. The contest hall is where you compete against other stylists to create an outfit that best suits the prompt you are given. An example of a prompt would be, "A bold look that will shock the world". You need to make sure that your clothing items are cohesive in style in order to win.

Objects, Music, and Sounds

The main object you interact with in Style Savvy: Trendsetters is your Cell Phone. Your Cell Phone includes many different features that provide you with useful information in order to improve gameplay. You can view pictures that you have taken in Albums, look up the many fashion terms mentioned throughout the game in Style Index, or keep up on the latest trends in the local magazine "Fashion Nuances". These are just a few of the features your cell phone is capable of. There is a lot of variety in the music for the game aswell, with each location having different types. You can also change the type of music that plays in your boutique to fit the atmosphere.

Conclusion

Although I love playing this game, I think there are certain things that can be improved if it were to be developed in the future. The gameplay can get a bit repetitive as it focuses on one gameplay loop. I also think the developers should expand more on the business aspect of the game. For example, allowing players to set their own prices and have more freedom when managing the details of their shop.
