Summer Internship Blog

Week One

My first week interning with Kate & Co. has been a very informative and exciting experience that has introduced me to the company’s day-to-day operations and how the team effectively collaborates to meet client needs. I got set up with many of the company’s platforms and tools they use to communicate and manage projects, which helped me to further my understanding of workflow processes and how tasks are assigned. I also had the opportunity to sit in on the weekly virtual all-team call, where I learned about ongoing projects and what each team member was currently working on. One of my favorite opportunities this week was contributing to some graphic design work for clients using Canva. Although my experience with Canva was quite limited, I was able to look through past designs and see how the team maintains brand consistency through the use of color schemes, typography, and layout. This hands-on experience allowed me to develop new graphic design skills and gave me the confidence to handle similar tasks in the future. Overall I’m very pleased with how my first week went and am excited for what’s to come!
